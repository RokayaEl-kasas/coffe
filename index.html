Mocha

It is believed that the drink was named mainly after the Mocha Bean, a type of coffee bean that was shipped from the Mocha Port in Yemen. Mocha beans were known and famous for their natural chocolate flavor. Now the name "Mocha" no longer refers to this type of coffee bean, but rather to the presence of chocolate flavors added to the coffee drink.

ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons Nescafe
  • 3 tablespoons milk powder, or coffee creamer
  • 3 tablespoons of sugar
  • 25 ice cubes

Mode of operation

  1. Put all the ingredients of the drink in an electric blender
  2. Mix the ingredients well and then pour into serving glasses
  3. Serve immediately
